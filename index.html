Fundamental Analysis Project

Click to view

As part of a solo project for the Finance and Economics Club at IIT Guwahati, I conducted a comprehensive fundamental analysis of various publicly listed companies, including Cipla Limited, MRF Limited, and ITC Limited. Using financial statements, ratio analysis, and industry trends, I evaluated the performance and growth prospects of these companies.


Key Highlights:

  • Cipla Limited: Analyzed growth in earnings, revenue, and return on equity. Based on metrics such as earnings per share (EPS) growth and sustainability ratings, I assigned a stock rating of 4/5.
  • MRF Limited: Reviewed the company's dominant position in the Indian tyre market, evaluating its revenue growth, EPS forecast, and return on equity, resulting in a stock rating of 4/5.
  • ITC Limited: Studied ITC’s diversified portfolio in the FMCG sector, with a focus on its brand portfolio and future prospects in the Indian consumer market, leading to a stock rating of 4/5.
This project allowed me to apply fundamental analysis techniques and develop insights into the financial health and potential of companies across different industries.

Tools Used: Ratio Analysis, Financial Report Review, Market Trend Analysis.

Skills Gained: Financial Analysis, Critical Thinking, Stock Rating, Research, Industry Evaluation.

Grocery Store Management Project

Click to view

In this project, I developed a Grocery Store Management System to streamline the process of managing customer orders and product inventory for a grocery store. The system features an intuitive user interface where store managers can manage customer orders and product details efficiently.

Key Features:

  • Order Management: Users can create, view, and manage orders, including details such as the order number, customer name, date, and total cost.
  • Product Management: The system allows for easy addition, removal, and updating of products within the store’s inventory.
  • Dashboard: Provides an overview of the order history with sortable fields like date, order number, and total cost, ensuring smooth operations.

The system was built with a focus on ease of use and efficiency, enhancing productivity for store management by automating routine tasks.

Technologies Used: Frontend (HTML/CSS/JavaScript), Backend (Python/Flask), Database (SQLite/MySQL).

Skills Gained: Full-Stack Development, Database Management, UI/UX Design, Order and Inventory Management Systems.
